### Interpretation

This analysis examines how sequence heterogeneity (composition entropy) manifests as geometric properties (backbone curvature, flexibility) in protein structures. A modest raw entropy-curvature correlation is observed, but the signal weakens under pLDDT filtering and length/confidence controls, so the molecular evidence should be treated as preliminary. Expanded coverage and higher-confidence segments are needed to assess whether mechanosensitive proteins show stronger coupling.
