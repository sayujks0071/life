# Research Timeline

A chronological view of the key publications and milestones in the development of the Biological Counter-Curvature (BCC) theory and its foundations.

## 2025

- **Biological Countercurvature of Spacetime (In Prep)**: Primary manuscript introducing the IEC-Cosserat framework.
- **AFDB Integration**: Mapping AlphaFold structures to mechanical parameters.

## 2024

- **AlphaFold 3 Release**: Enabling accurate structure prediction of biomolecular interactions (Abramson et al.).

## 2023

- **PyElastica Zenodo Release**: Standardizing open-source simulations of Cosserat rods.

## 2021

- **AlphaFold 2 (Nature)**: Breakthrough in protein structure prediction (Jumper et al.).
- **Elastica Soft Robotics (IEEE)**: Expanding rod models to compliant environments.

## 2018-2019

- **Modeling Complex Musculoskeletal Architectures (Nat. Comm.)**: Zhang et al. apply rod theory to biology.
- **Spinal Changes in Microgravity (J. Physiol.)**: Green & Scott review the impact of loading on spinal health.

## 2013-2016

- **Zebrafish Scoliosis (Science)**: Grimes et al. link CSF flow to spine curvature (2016).
- **Morphoelastic Rods (JMPS)**: Moulton et al. formalize growing elastic structures (2013).

## Pre-2010

- **Vertebrate Segmentation (Cell)**: Pourquie (2011) connects gene networks to scoliosis.
- **Hox Patterning (Dev. Dyn.)**: Wellik (2007) reviews axial skeleton patterning.
- **Clinical Biomechanics (White & Panjabi)**: The 1990 foundational text on spine mechanics.
