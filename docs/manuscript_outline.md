## 1. Abstract & Key Points
*   **Problem:** The spine is often modeled as failing under gravity. We propose it actively develops "Anti-Gravity" geometry.
*   **Hypothesis:** Curvature is a programmed, gene-regulated solution to the optimization problem of upright posture in a 1g field.
*   **Methods:** Synthesis of cilia genetics, HOX patterning, Cosserat rod mechanics, and AlphaFold structural data.
*   **Conclusion:** Spinal deformities (scoliosis) are "decoding errors" in this counter-curvature program.
